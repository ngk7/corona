PREVENTION

Preventive measures to reduce the chances of infection include,
  1. Staying at home avoiding crowded places.
  2. Washing hands with soap and warm water often and for at least 20 seconds.
  3. Practicing good respiratory hygiene and avoiding touching the eyes, nose, or mouth with unwashed hands.
  4. The CDC recommends covering the mouth and nose with a tissue when coughing or sneezing, or using inside of the elbow if no tissue is available.
  5. They also recommend proper hand hygiene after any cough or sneeze.
  6. Social distancing strategies aim to reduce contact of infected persons with large groups by closing schools and workplaces, restricting travel, and canceling mass gatherings.
  7. Social distancing also includes that people stay at least 6 feet apart (about 1.80 meters).

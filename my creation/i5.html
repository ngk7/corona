TREATMENT

The treatment may involve:

  • fluids to reduce the risk of dehydration
  • medication to reduce a fever
  • supplemental oxygen in more severe cases
Some treatment options that are currently being investigated for protection against SARS-CoV-2 and treatment of COVID-19 symptoms are as follows:
  1. Remdesivir:Researchers have found that remdesivir is highly effective at fighting the novel coronavirus in isolated cells.
  2. Chloroquine:It is discovered that this drug is effective at fighting the SARS-CoV-2 virus in studies done in test tubes.
  3. Lopinavir and ritonavir:In South Korea, a 54-year-old man was given a combination of these two drugs and had a significant reductionTrusted Source in his levels of the coronavirus.
  4. Favilavir:China has approved the use of the antiviral drug favilavir to treat symptoms of COVID-19. The drug was initially developed to treat inflammation in the nose and throat.
